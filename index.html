Frequently Asked Questions

What does ParalleloCamera do?
ParalleloCamera captures photos and videos from both the front and back cameras simultaneously.

Why does the app need camera access?
Camera access is required to let the app capture photos and videos.

Why does the app need microphone access?
Microphone access is required when recording videos with sound.

Where are my photos and videos stored?
Your captured content is stored on your device unless you choose to share it.
