Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ParalleloCamera handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

ParalleloCamera does not collect personal information directly unless you contact us by email.

Camera and Microphone Access

ParalleloCamera requires access to your device’s camera to capture photos and videos using the front and back cameras. If you record videos with audio, the app also requires access to the microphone.

Photos and Videos

Photos and videos created with ParalleloCamera are stored on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your photos or videos on our servers.

Sharing

Any sharing of photos or videos happens only when you choose to do so using your device’s sharing features.

Third-Party Services

ParalleloCamera does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external data collection services unless otherwise stated in a future update.

Children’s Privacy

ParalleloCamera does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
